My Projects

Brochure Design for College Fest

The project is about the creation of a visually appealing and informative brochure for the cultural fest of IIIT Ranchi, designed by a team of students. The brochure showcases various events, performances, and activities that will take place during the fest. The goal is to promote the cultural fest and encourage student participation while contributing to the campus community.With our collective skills in UI/UX design, we worked to create a design that is both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly.

Travel and Tourism website

This is a personal project on UI/UX design for travel and tourism. I aim to create a user-friendly platform for travelers to plan and book trips, prioritizing safety and comfort. With the pandemic affecting travel, I want to offer a fresh approach. I hope to showcase my design skills and create a tool to help people explore the world and create memories.

Resturant Landing Page

Here is a personal UI/UX design project for restaurants and food. The goal is to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for browsing menus, ordering food, and making reservations. The design emphasizes the uniqueness of each restaurant, while also creating a seamless and enjoyable experience for users. The project aims to help restaurants connect with customers and users to discover new restaurants and cuisines, making the dining experience engaging and memorable.

Budget Tracking App

As a young adult and designer, I understand the challenges of managing finances and staying within a budget.So I designed a budget tracking app designed for youth.It aims to create a user-friendly and visually appealing interface that will help young people track their expenses and make better financial decisions. Through this project, I hope to showcase my skills in UI/UX design while creating a tool that can help young people become more financially responsible and independent.

Posters

Posters have a unique ability to catch people's eye and convey a message in a way that other mediums may not be able to. As a designer, I love the challenge of creating posters that are not only visually appealing but also effectively communicate the intended message to the viewer, whether it's through typography, imagery, or color.

Daily UI Challenge

As a designer, I enjoy participating in the Daily UI Challenge, which is a design challenge that requires me to create a user interface (UI) design every day for 100 days. The challenge is an excellent way to develop my skills and boost my creativity by providing me with a different prompt or task each day, ranging from designing a dashboard to creating a checkout form..Participating in the Daily UI Challenge has been a fantastic experience for me as a designer, helping me to experiment with different design techniques and UI elements. The challenge has pushed me to think outside the box and come up with unique design solutions, improving my skills in UI/UX design.
